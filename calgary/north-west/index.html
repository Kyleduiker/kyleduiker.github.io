Calgary NW

Academic Roots, Natural Surroundings

Northwest Calgary is anchored by the University of Calgary and Foothills Medical Centre, making it a hub for students, medical professionals, and families.

The quadrant ranges from established inner-city communities like Capitol Hill to newer northwest suburbs, offering a wide spectrum of home styles and price points.
