Why Choose Calgary

Calgary offers an exceptional quality of life combining economic opportunity, natural beauty, and urban sophistication. Discover what makes Calgary one of Canada's most desirable cities to call home.

Strong Economy

Home to Canada's energy sector and a diversifying economy with opportunities in tech, finance, and entrepreneurship. Calgary offers competitive salaries and low unemployment rates.

No Provincial Sales Tax

Alberta has no PST, meaning more money in your pocket. Combined with competitive property taxes, Calgary offers exceptional value for homeowners.

Affordable Housing

Compared to Toronto and Vancouver, Calgary offers significantly more affordable housing options with larger homes and properties at accessible price points.

Outdoor Recreation

World-class skiing, hiking, and mountain biking just an hour away. Fish Creek Provincial Park, countless pathways, and year-round outdoor activities at your doorstep.

Top Education

Excellent public and private schools, plus top-tier post-secondary institutions including the University of Calgary and Mount Royal University.

Vibrant Culture

World-famous Calgary Stampede, thriving arts scene, diverse culinary options, professional sports, and year-round festivals celebrating Calgary's multicultural community.

Explore Calgary by Quadrant

Each of Calgary's quadrants offers its own unique character, amenities, and lifestyle

Southeast Calgary

Master-planned communities with lakes, new schools, and modern amenities. Home to Seton, Mahogany, Auburn Bay, and South Health Campus.

Southwest Calgary

Established neighborhoods close to downtown and the mountains. Features parks, top schools, and easy access to outdoor recreation.

Northwest Calgary

Family-friendly communities with excellent schools, shopping, and quick access to both downtown and the mountains via Trans-Canada Highway.

Northeast Calgary

Diverse, multicultural communities with affordable housing options. Close to the airport, major employers, and growing commercial districts.

Calgary Lifestyle

Everything you need for an exceptional quality of life

Recreation & Outdoor

  • Fish Creek Provincial Park - one of Canada's largest urban parks
  • 1000+ km of pathways and bike trails
  • World-class skiing 90 minutes away (Banff, Lake Louise)
  • Bowness Park, Prince's Island Park, and countless green spaces
  • Bow and Elbow Rivers for fishing and kayaking
  • Year-round golf courses and recreation facilities

Arts & Culture

  • Calgary Stampede - "The Greatest Outdoor Show on Earth"
  • National Music Centre and Studio Bell
  • Glenbow Museum and Heritage Park
  • Calgary Philharmonic Orchestra
  • Thriving theatre and arts district (East Village)
  • Year-round festivals and events

Shopping & Dining

  • CORE Shopping Centre downtown
  • CrossIron Mills - Alberta's largest outlet mall
  • Chinook Centre, Market Mall, Southcentre
  • 17th Avenue SW - trendy restaurants and boutiques
  • Kensington Village, Inglewood, and Mission districts
  • Diverse culinary scene from fine dining to food trucks

Sports & Entertainment

  • Calgary Flames (NHL) at Scotiabank Saddledome
  • Calgary Stampeders (CFL) at McMahon Stadium
  • Calgary Roughnecks (NLL) lacrosse
  • WinSport - Olympic training facility open to public
  • Telus Convention Centre for major events
  • Year-round festivals, concerts, and entertainment
