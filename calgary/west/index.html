Calgary West

Mature Communities, Foothills Character

West Calgary is defined by its well-established neighbourhoods, generous lot sizes, and easy access to both the city centre and the Rocky Mountains. Communities here have a settled, leafy feel.

Buyers are drawn to Calgary West for its school options, transit corridors, and the outdoor lifestyle that comes with proximity to Nose Hill Park, Edworthy Park, and the Bow River pathway system.

Close proximity to Kananaskis and Banff
Mature neighbourhoods with established trees
Access to strong school communities
Mix of single-family homes and condos

Neighbourhoods to Know

  • Signal Hill — established, family-friendly, great views
  • Cougar Ridge — newer development, mountain proximity
  • West Springs — upscale, growing community hub
  • Strathcona Park — mature, quiet, central west
  • Aspen Woods — premium homes, top school catchments
  • Springbank Hill — semi-rural feel, luxury properties
