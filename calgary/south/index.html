Calgary South

Schools, Stability, South Calgary

The South quadrant has long been a destination for families prioritizing school catchments, community character, and the kind of established infrastructure that comes with decades of thoughtful development.

From the inner-city charm of Altadore and Marda Loop to the mature suburbs of Shawnessy and Evergreen, South Calgary covers a broad range of lifestyles and price points.

Consistently top-ranked school communities
Easy access to MacLeod Trail and Glenmore Trail
Variety of housing from bungalows to modern infills
Strong long-term resale performance

Neighbourhoods to Know

  • Altadore / Marda Loop — inner-city, walkable, vibrant
  • Bridlewood — family suburb, great school access
  • Evergreen — established, quiet, amenity-complete
  • Shawnessy — transit-connected, established retail hub
  • Oakridge — mature trees, solid single-family stock
  • Lake Bonavista — Calgary's original lake community
