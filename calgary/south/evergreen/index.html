Community Overview

Living in Evergreen, Calgary

Evergreen is located in South Calgary, positioned just south of Fish Creek Provincial Park and west of Macleod Trail, offering access to both natural space and key commuting routes.

Housing in Evergreen typically ranges from the mid $200Ks to over $800K+, including apartments, townhomes, and detached homes across different phases of development. Pricing varies based on condition, location, and upgrades. Verify with current MLS® listings.

The community offers a balance of space, access, and amenities, making it a consistent option for buyers looking for a practical South Calgary location with established infrastructure and ongoing growth.

Lifestyle & Vibe

What's It Like to Live in Evergreen?

Daily life in Evergreen is built around convenience and accessibility, with nearby shopping, schools, and services supporting day-to-day living without long drives.

The surrounding retail and transit options make it easy to manage daily routines without travelling far — Shawnessy Shopping Centre, nearby LRT stations, and a range of local services cover most needs.

Before You Decide

Things to Consider in Evergreen

Every community has trade-offs. Here's what buyers should weigh before committing to Evergreen.

!
Commute times can increase during peak periods along Macleod Trail — plan for variability during morning and evening rush.
!
Different phases of development create variation in home styles and pricing, so buyers should compare carefully within the community rather than using a single benchmark.
!
LRT access requires a short drive to nearby Shawnessy or Somerset-Bridlewood stations — there is no station within walking distance.
!
The larger community layout means some amenities are not within easy walking distance, making a car necessary for most daily errands.
!
Price points vary widely depending on property type and location within Evergreen, so buyers should define their segment before searching.
