Discover Mahogany

Mahogany is Calgary's largest private lake community, offering an exceptional lakefront lifestyle in Southeast Calgary. Spanning over 4,000 acres, this master-planned community features three private beaches, extensive wetlands, and a vibrant year-round lifestyle centered around water, nature, and community connection.

Developed by Genstar Development Company, Mahogany combines the tranquility of lakeside living with urban convenience. Residents enjoy exclusive access to white sand beaches, a resident-only Beach Club, and miles of scenic pathways - all while being just minutes from major shopping, dining, and entertainment at Seton Urban District and South Trail Crossing.

Whether you're a young family seeking a tight-knit community, active retirees looking for year-round recreation, or professionals wanting the perfect work-life balance, Mahogany offers a unique Calgary lifestyle that brings resort living to your doorstep.

The Mahogany Lifestyle

Experience resort-style living every day in Southeast Calgary's most sought-after community

Lakefront Living

Three pristine freshwater lakes with private white sand beaches exclusively for Mahogany residents. Enjoy swimming, kayaking, paddleboarding, and beach volleyball all summer long.

Beach Club

The exclusive Mahogany Beach Club features a restaurant, ice cream parlor, equipment rentals, and event spaces. It's the heart of community life and summer fun.

Year-Round Events

From summer beach parties and outdoor movies to winter festivals and skating, Mahogany's community association hosts events that bring neighbors together throughout the year.

Prime Location

Minutes from Seton Urban District, South Trail Crossing, and South Health Campus. Easy access to Stoney Trail and Deerfoot Trail puts all of Calgary within reach.

Top Schools

Mahogany is served by several excellent public and Catholic schools, with more being built as the community grows. Perfect for families with children of all ages.

Diverse Housing

From luxury lakefront estates to affordable starter homes, townhouses, and condos, Mahogany offers housing options for every stage of life and budget.
