City Centre

Urban Living, Downtown Energy

The City Centre quadrant encompasses Calgary's downtown core and surrounding inner-city neighbourhoods — from Beltline's condo towers to the charming streets of Kensington and Inglewood.

Condos, lofts, and townhomes are the primary property types here, appealing to young professionals, downsizers, and investors looking for central location and walkability.

Walkable to offices, restaurants, and the Bow River Pathway
Condo and loft-style living options
Close to cultural venues and entertainment
Strong rental demand for investors

Neighbourhoods to Know

  • Beltline — high-density condo living, vibrant streets
  • Kensington — boutique shops, cafes, community feel
  • Eau Claire — riverside living, peace in the city
  • Inglewood — Calgary's oldest neighbourhood, artsy vibe
  • East Village — revitalized, contemporary urban hub
  • Bridgeland — charming, walkable, local favourites
