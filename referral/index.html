The Problem

Choosing the wrong agent in a new city is a real risk

Most people relocating out of Calgary do the same thing: they search online, skim a few profiles, check the star ratings, and hope for the best.

But reviews don't tell the full story. They don't tell you how an agent handles a difficult negotiation, whether they communicate clearly under pressure, or whether they'll actually pick up the phone when it matters.

A bad introduction doesn't just cost you time — it can cost you money, create unnecessary stress, and leave you without a trusted advocate at a critical moment.

Online reviews reflect satisfaction — not competence under pressure or in a challenging negotiation.
Without a personal introduction, you're starting from scratch — no context, no accountability, no advocate.

Once you've left Calgary, there's no one managing the relationship or ensuring the service level stays where it should be.
